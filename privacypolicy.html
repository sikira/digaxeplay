How YTs Play Uses Cookies and Similar Technologies

A. Cookie

A cookie is a text file stored by a web server on a computer or mobile device, and the content of a cookie can be retrieved and read only by the server that created the cookie. Cookies are unique to the browser or mobile application you are using. The text in a cookie often consists of identifiers, site names, and some numbers and characters.

Sometimes, YTs Play stores cookies on computers or mobile devices for the purpose of improving user experience, including the following scenarios:

(1) Technical cookies: Login and verification. When you use your account to login to youtube, the "session-based" cookies ensure that your visit to this site functions as smoothly as possible. YTs Play Browser allow youtube.com to store cookies.

(2) Personalization cookies: Storage of your preferences and settings. A website can use cookies to save settings, such as the language setting and font size on your computer or mobile device, and other browser preferences. YTs Play allows youtube.com to store personalization cookies.

(3) Advertising cookies. YTs Play does NOT uses cookies to collect information about your online activities and interests and provide you with advertisements, but YTs Play allows youtube.com to store advertising cookies.

(4) Statistical cookies. With cookies, YTs Play does NOT collect information about your use of our websites and other applications, either for a single visit (using a session cookie) or for repeated visits (using a persistent cookie). YTs Play allows youtube.com to store statistical cookies.

(5) Social Media Cookies. Social media cookies are NOT linked to services provided by third parties, such as 'Like' buttons and 'Share' buttons. The third party provides these services in return for recognizing that you have visited our websites. YTs Play allows youtube.com to store social media cookies

(5) Youtube and Google Cookies policy. For information how Youtube and Google manage your cookies, review there privacy-policy

You can't manage or delete cookies at your own preference(verison 1.0, from version 1.1 you will have that option). For details, visit AboutCookies.org. However, blocking cookies will require you to change your user settings every time you open the youtube.

If you clear cookies, you will need to change your settings the next time you visit YTs Play app.

Disabling cookies may affect your use of some or all functions of these services.
