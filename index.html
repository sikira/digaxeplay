YTs Play

Download from official store and enjoy

YTs Play has been created with the purpose of getting the original YouTube experience on your Huawei device.

Application doesn't require any Google Play services to run smoothly, but you can always sign in with your youtube account for full experience.


Features

You can enjoy original experience like
- see recommended videos
- your subscriptions
- manage your account
- subscribe to your favorite channels
- create native youtube playlist
- like, share and comment videos you love
- browse your original youtube history ( also clear and remove videos from it )
- and many more.

Personal information

YTs Play application does NOT collect, store, analyse or share any of your personal information, or your login credentials ( username or password ), and it will NEVER do so!

For ultimate user experience YTs Play is free from in-app advertisement, only youtube native ads are showing.

Create and play playlist

Add videos to watch later playlist or create custome youtube playlists. Delete and manage playlist.

Search any content

User can choose to search content on home page, trending page, recommended page or to search his history and playlist.

Search while playing

Video can be viewed as floating window, and allow user to search for new content.

Enjoy content on fullscreen

Enjoy videos in fullscreen mode. Depending on device content can be watch in wide or compact mode.
